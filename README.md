# Hi there!  <img src="https://github.com/TheDudeThatCode/TheDudeThatCode/blob/master/Assets/Hi.gif" width="35" height="35">

Welcome to my Github! I am currently an Automation Engineer at Oceaneering Mobile Robotics (OMR). We develop and deploy fleets of counterbalance forklifts, underriders and fork-over-leg vehicles to meet our US customer's material handling automation needs. 

Prior to OMR, I was a Graduate student at the University of Maryland, College Park, pursuing my Master's in Robotics.I had taken advanced coursework in areas like Robot Planning, Perception, Simulation, Machine Learning, AI, and Neural Networks.


Here, you will find some of the programming projects I have worked on through coursework and in my spare time.
